DRINKS EXPLAINED GUIDE

How do you make the infusions?

Whiskey Infused.

Our coffee beans are soaked in Honey Jack Daniels - whiskey - for 48 hours after they are roasted, We highlight the barrel and smoky cinnamon flavors.

Rum Infused.

Our coffee beans are soaked in Don Q Rum for 48 hours after they are roasted, we created a secret & delicious recipe with a few highlighted notes.

Tequila Infused.

Our coffee beans are soaked in Don Julio for 48 hours after they are roasted, Citrus and brown sugar notes are highlighted for robust and elegant coffee taste

About Strawberry Mocha Draft Latte

Meet Strawberry Mocha Draft Latte, our newest flavor to fall for. Subtly sweet strawberry combines with the luscious flavors of a cafe mocha to create a seasonal cold-textured latte you won't want to put down. Made with natural flavors, real farm-fresh milk, real cold brew made from coffee beans roasted by us, and the texture of a real cafe latte. Best enjoyed chilled!

  • 100 mg natural caffeine (1 cup of coffee)
